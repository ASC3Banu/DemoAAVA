1. INTRODUCTION
================================================================================

1.1 Document Purpose
--------------------
This High-Level Design (HLD) document provides a comprehensive architectural blueprint for the AI-Powered Logistics Monitoring System. It translates business requirements and architectural decisions from ADR AD-1 into a detailed technical design that guides implementation teams.

The document serves as:
- A technical reference for development teams
- An architectural baseline for solution delivery
- A traceability artifact linking requirements to design decisions
- A compliance and governance artifact for architecture review boards

1.2 Scope and Objectives
------------------------
Scope:
The system encompasses a global logistics monitoring platform that enables:
- Real-time tracking of shipments across multiple regions
- AI-powered predictive analytics for delay detection
- Automated alert and escalation management
- Operational dashboards for performance insights
- Integration with ports
